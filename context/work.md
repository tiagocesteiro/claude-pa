## Business Ideas

### 1. Global Design, Local Production (3D Printing Marketplace) [PRIMARY FOCUS]
A decentralized marketplace connecting customers with local 3D printer owners.
- Curated catalog of CC-licensed models (e.g., MakerWorld)
- Geolocation matching to nearest printer (reduces CO2 + shipping costs)
- Service layer: custom file uploads + designer consultancy
- Built-in licensing engine for creator/maker compliance
- Tagline: "Click Globally, Print Locally"

### 2. 7-Eleven Smoothie (Frozen Smoothie Vending) [SECONDARY FOCUS]
Automated self-service smoothie model for Lisbon gyms and co-working spaces.
- Pre-packed frozen cups blended in 60s using HM-160E tabletop machine
- Cost ~€1.20/cup | Retail €4.50–€5.50
- Partner model: 20-30% profit share with venue
- PLA compostable cups (compliant with 2026 single-use plastic ban)
- Target locations: Vertigo, Escala25 (gyms) | IDEA Spaces, Avila Spaces (co-working)
