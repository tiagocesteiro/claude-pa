## TOPIC 1: Legal & Compliance in Portugal

**Bottom line: No single requirement blocks launch. Total cost: €50–€300.**

### Step-by-Step Process

| Step | What | Where | Cost | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | NIF registration (tax ID) | portaldasfinancas.gov.pt | FREE | 1–2 weeks |
| 2 | Food hygiene certificate (Nível I) | CTL +351 21 793 0000 / IEFP | €100–€120 | 1–2 days |
| 3 | ASAE registration | asae.gov.pt (online) | FREE | 5–10 days |
| 4 | CML municipal license (confirm if needed) | +351 21 882 0000 | FREE to confirm | 1 day call |
| 5 | EN 13432 certificate | Cup supplier provides it | FREE | On order |

### Notes

**ASAE Food Business Registration:**
- Register online at www.asae.gov.pt
- Requires NIF first
- FREE — just a notification process
- Inspection happens after, typically 1–2 weeks

**Food Hygiene Certificate:**
- "Nível I" certificate required for food handling
- Duration: 1–2 days (often online)
- Providers: CTL (+351 21 793 0000), IEFP (subsidized), IQQA
- Cost: €50–€150

**PLA Cups & 2026 Plastic Ban:**
- Law: Decreto-Lei 68-A/2020 (effective January 1, 2026)
- PLA cups with EN 13432 certification are exempt/compliant
- EN 13432 = the cup supplier's responsibility to certify — NOT yours
- Just ask supplier for the certificate document with your order

**Municipal License (CML):**
- Likely NOT required if machine is inside a private gym/co-work (covered by venue's license)
- Confirm with one call to +351 21 882 0000

**Critical:** Get written authorization from the venue first — ASAE registration references the venue address.
