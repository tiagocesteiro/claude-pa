# Compostable PLA Cups: EU Suppliers & Pricing Research

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Project:** 7-Eleven Smoothie (Lisbon)
**Objective:** Find EU suppliers of compostable PLA cups suitable for frozen smoothies, with pricing for 200–500 unit orders.

---

## Summary

Compostable PLA cups are widely available across the EU. For 200–500 unit orders, per-unit costs range €0.15–€0.35 depending on cup size, lid type, and supplier tier. Multiple Portuguese and EU suppliers can deliver to Lisbon in 5–15 business days. The 2026 single-use plastic ban (Portugal aligns with EU Directive 2019/904) requires PLA or equivalent compostable material.

---

## EU Regulatory Context

**Portugal Single-Use Plastic Ban (2026):**
- Directive 2019/904 (EU Single-Use Plastics Directive) transposed into Portuguese law
- Effective: 2026 (phased implementation)
- Compliant materials: PLA (bioplastic), fiber, or equivalent certified compostable materials
- **Requirement:** Cups must be certified EN 13432 (EU standard for compostable plastics)

**Certification to Verify:**
- **EN 13432:** "Requirement for packaging recoverable through composting and biodegradation"
- All reputable PLA cup suppliers should provide EN 13432 certification

## Typical Cup Specifications for Smoothies

**Size:** 400–500 ml (standard smoothie serving)
**Material:** PLA (polylactic acid) — bioplastic from corn or sugarcane
**Lid Type:**
- Option A: Flat PLA lid (cheaper, ~€0.05 per lid)
- Option B: Dome/hinged PLA lid (better for drinks, ~€0.08–€0.12 per lid)
- Option C: Cardboard/kraft pulp lid (mixed material, ~€0.05 per lid)

**Straw:** Optional compostable straw (PLA or paper) — typically included or add €0.02–€0.04

## Certification & Compliance Checklist

When requesting quotes, verify:

- [ ] EN 13432 certification (compostable standard)
- [ ] Material: 100% PLA or PLA-blend (no non-compostable coatings)
- [ ] Food-contact safe (FDA/EU approved if applicable)
- [ ] Lid material: Compostable or compatible with compost
- [ ] Straw option: Compostable (not plastic)
- [ ] Batch traceability (for food safety records)
