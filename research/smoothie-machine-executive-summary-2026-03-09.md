# 7-Eleven Smoothie — Executive Summary & Next Steps

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Project:** Frozen Smoothie Vending for Lisbon (Gyms & Co-Working)
**Objective:** Consolidate research findings across equipment, ingredients, packaging, and regulations. Identify highest-priority actions for feasibility validation.

## TL;DR — Is This Viable?

**YES — with 4–8 week lead time for regulatory approval + supplier setup.**

Core economics work (70–85% margin), EU suppliers exist at scale, and ASAE regulations are manageable for a small operation. Main risks: venue approval (need to confirm gyms/co-working allow vending), demand forecasting (unknown daily cup sales), and supplier reliability (no confirmed Portuguese distributors yet).

**Go/No-Go Decision Date:** March 30, 2026 (after supplier quotes + venue responses)

## Supplier Selection Criteria (Scoring)

**Equipment (HM-160E):**
- Price (€350–€500 target)
- Lead time (<30 days preferred)
- Warranty (12+ months)
- Support availability (critical for troubleshooting)

**PLA Cups:**
- Price (<€0.25/unit for 500+ order)
- EN 13432 certification (mandatory)
- Lead time to Lisbon (<15 days)
- Lids included (vs. separate order)

**Frozen Smoothie Base:**
- Price (€0.35–€0.50 per 400ml pack)
- Flavor variety (3+ options)
- Lead time to Lisbon (<10 days)
- Storage flexibility (Portugal temperature variation)

| Phase | Timeline | Cost | Effort |

| ASAE inspection | 2–3 weeks (after notification) | €0 | MEDIUM (venue prep) |

## Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Venue declines vending | Project blocked | Contact venues immediately (week 1); have 4+ options |
| ASAE rejects FBA | 8-week delay | Prepare documentation meticulously; engage venue early |
| Machine defects/support issues | Revenue loss | Choose supplier with warranty + demo unit first |
| PLA cup quality (brittle in freezer) | Product failure | Test sample cups with machine before bulk order |
| Demand forecasting (cups/day uncertain) | Cash flow risk | Start 1 venue, validate demand, then scale |
| Supplier reliability (frozen base unpredictable) | Supply chain failure | Identify 2–3 backup suppliers |
