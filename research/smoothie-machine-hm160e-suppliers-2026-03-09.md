## EU Supplier Options

### Option 1: Alibaba (Direct from China)
**Supplier Type:** Multiple manufacturers
**Sourcing Method:** Alibaba B2B marketplace (1688.com or Alibaba.com)

**Typical Pricing:**
- Unit cost: €200–€400 (for single unit or small batch, 1–5 units)
- MOQ: Often 1–10 units
- Lead time: 20–40 days by sea, 7–10 days by express

**Estimated Total Cost to Portugal (Single Unit):**
- Machine: ~€350 (mid-range quote)
- Express shipping (DHL/FedEx): ~€80–€150
- **Total: ~€430–€500**

**Pros:**
- Lowest unit cost
- Flexible MOQ

**Cons:**
- Longer lead times (sea freight)
- No local support
- Risk of quality variance
- Currency/payment complexity

**ACTION:** Search Alibaba for "HM-160E blender" or "commercial smoothie blender" and request quotes from 3–5 suppliers.
