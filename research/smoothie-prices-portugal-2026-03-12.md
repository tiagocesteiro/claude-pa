# Smoothie Prices in Portugal — Market Research
**Date:** 2026-03-12
**Purpose:** Evaluate whether €5.00 is a realistic retail price for a self-service frozen smoothie vending machine in Lisbon gyms and co-working spaces.

---

## Summary

A 400–500ml fresh or artisan smoothie in Lisbon costs €4–€9 depending on venue and ingredients. Supermarket ready-to-drink is €0.99–€1.79 for 250–330ml. The €5.00 price point for a vending machine smoothie sits comfortably inside the local market range — above budget supermarket but below premium juice bars. No self-service smoothie vending operation was found operating in Portugal as of this research.

## 2. Gyms and Fitness Studios

No gym in Lisbon was found with published smoothie prices on their website. This channel is largely word-of-mouth / in-venue only. The lack of published pricing is itself a data point — gyms either use vending machines for protein shakes or have very limited bar service.

The Designhunger 2025 Lisbon Fitness Guide notes that **Aura Wellness Bar** (at mvmt.studio) offers "drinks, fresh food and breakfast plates" — but prices are not listed.

**Inference from comparable European markets (not verified for Lisbon specifically):** Gym smoothie bars in Spain and UK typically charge €5–€8 for fresh smoothies. This is consistent with the Açaí Natura pricing found in Lisbon.

## 4. Self-Service / Vending Smoothie Concepts in Portugal

**None found operating in Portugal.**

- Smoodi (US-based, Boston) is the closest concept — self-serve, frozen cups, 60-second blend — but operates only in the USA (gyms, convenience stores, universities). No Portugal presence found.
- NVending (nvending.net) is Portugal's leading vending operator but handles standard beverage/snack machines, not smoothie blending.
- No local competitor in the self-service blended smoothie space was identified.

## 6. Verdict: Is €5.00 Realistic?

**Yes — €5.00 is a defensible and well-positioned price point.**

- It is at the top of the budget/mid-tier street bar range (YAO: €3.90–€5.90)
- It is significantly below the premium brand tier (Açaí Natura: €7.90–€8.26)
- It is 3–5x more than supermarket ready-to-drink — but the freshly-blended / self-service experience justifies this
- In a gym or co-working context, customers are already spending €30–€80/month on membership; a €5 smoothie is a natural impulse purchase
- The self-service novelty factor (60-second blend, frozen cups) supports the premium vs. a canned drink from a standard vending machine

**Risk:** If the machine is placed next to a protein shake bar selling €4 shakes, pricing parity pressure could be an issue. Consider testing at €4.50 initially in co-working spaces and €5.00 in gyms.
