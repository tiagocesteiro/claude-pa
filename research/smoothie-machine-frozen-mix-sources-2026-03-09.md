## Option 3: Hybrid Model (Recommended for MVP)

**Strategy:** Buy pre-made frozen bases + add fresh local fruit

**Example Mix:**
- Pre-made berry base: в‚¬0.40 (400 ml, frozen pack)
- Fresh banana slice: в‚¬0.10 (add to base)

- **Total: ~в‚¬0.65 per cup** (good margin vs. в‚¬4.50вЂ“в‚¬5.50 retail)

## Freezer Space Requirements

**Per-Cup Analysis:**
- 1 frozen smoothie cup: ~500 ml (0.0005 mВі)
- Daily sales projection (1 venue): 20вЂ“50 cups
- Stock needed (3-day buffer): 60вЂ“150 cups = 0.03вЂ“0.075 mВі

**Equipment Options:**

1. **Chest Freezer (Standard Commercial)**
   - Capacity: 200вЂ“300 liters (0.2вЂ“0.3 mВі)
   - Temperature: -18В°C
   - Cost: в‚¬200вЂ“в‚¬400 (one-time purchase)

2. **Under-Counter Freezer (Space-Efficient)**
   - Capacity: 100вЂ“150 liters (0.1вЂ“0.15 mВі)
   - Cost: в‚¬300вЂ“в‚¬500
   - Can store: ~200вЂ“300 packs

## Supply Chain Management

**Ordering Frequency:**
- Pre-made packs: Weekly orders (5вЂ“10 day shelf life frozen)
- Fresh fruit: 2вЂ“3 times per week (market visits or distributor deliveries)
- Milk/add-ins: 1вЂ“2 times per week

**Inventory Turnover:**
- Target: 3вЂ“5 day inventory cycle
- Reduces waste, keeps product fresh
- Requires consistent demand forecasting

**Supplier Contact Checklist:**
- Minimum order size (MOQ)
- Lead time to Lisbon
- Storage requirements (temperature, humidity)
- Return/quality guarantee
- Payment terms (net 15, net 30, or COD)
