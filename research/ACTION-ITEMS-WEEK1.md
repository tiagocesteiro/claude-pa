## DOCUMENTATION PREP — All Week

### Action 8: HACCP Plan Outline

**TASK:** Prepare simplified HACCP document (1–2 pages) for ASAE submission

**WHAT GOES IN (Template):**

```
FOOD BUSINESS: [Business Name]
PRODUCT: Frozen Smoothie Vending Machine
LOCATION: [Venue Name, Address]

HAZARD ANALYSIS:
1. Temperature control failure
   - CCP: Freezer temperature monitoring
   - Control: Daily thermometer check, -18°C minimum
   - Records: Temperature log sheet

2. Allergen cross-contamination
   - CCP: Ingredient segregation
   - Control: Separate storage, labeled containers
   - Records: Allergen matrix, supplier certifications

3. Equipment hygiene
   - CCP: Blender cleaning
   - Control: Daily cleaning protocol
   - Records: Cleaning checklist

DOCUMENTATION:
- Temperature logs (weekly)
- Cleaning records (daily)
- Ingredient supplier list + allergen info
- Staff training records
```
