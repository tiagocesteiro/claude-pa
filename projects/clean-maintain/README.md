# Clean & Maintain — Gym Ergometer Cleaning & Maintenance

**Status:** Active — Phase 1 (Research complete, building starter kit, first outreach)
**Last updated:** 2026-03-11
**Goal:** First paying client by end of April 2026

---

## Concept

B2B fitness facility maintenance service, starting with ergometers (Concept2 rowers, ski ergs, bike ergs, Assault Bikes) and expanding to barbells, dumbbells, and kettlebells.

**The gap:** No Concept2-specialized erg maintenance service exists in Portugal. The UK has Rowgear (£70/machine) and the USA has Erg Doctor. Nothing equivalent in Lisbon.

**Target clients:**
- CrossFit boxes (Lisbon has 16 affiliated boxes)
- Boutique fitness studios (~150-200 in Lisbon)
- Condo and corporate gyms

**CAE codes:** 81220 (Specialized Cleaning) + 33190 (Equipment Repair)

---

## Revenue Model

| Model | Price | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Monthly retainer | €15-25/machine/month | Target model — recurring revenue |
| Per visit (ad hoc) | €80-150/visit | Good for first clients, no commitment |
| Annual contract | €800-1,500/year | Covers X visits + callouts |

**Example:** CrossFit box with 8 ergs = €120-200/month on retainer
